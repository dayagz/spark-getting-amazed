The Event-Sourced Queryable State Machine (EQSM) pattern, when combined with cloud technologies and machine learning capabilities, offers a comprehensive solution for managing and optimizing business processes. By incorporating resource forecasting, organizations can efficiently allocate cloud resources, ensure system scalability, and balance performance and cost-effectiveness.

As businesses continue to embrace the public cloud, EQSM provides the necessary framework to handle complex and evolving business processes. With enhanced visibility, SLA tracking, stakeholder notifications, predictive insights, and resource forecasting, EQSM equips organizations with the tools to thrive in the dynamic and demanding landscape of modern business environments.
